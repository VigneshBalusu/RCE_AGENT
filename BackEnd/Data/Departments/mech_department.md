## 2. Vision & Mission (Mechanical)
* **Search Tags:** **MECH_VISION, MECH_MISSION, MECH_GOALS.**

### Vision
"To become a **Centre of Excellence** in the field of Mechanical Engineering by providing quality technical education and research to learners and solve social and environmental problems by developing **innovative and creative skills**."

### Mission
1.  **Technical Expertise:** To empower students with innovative and research skills to attain opportunities in the Mechanical Engineering field.
2.  **Solution Providers:** To make graduates solution providers with a lifelong learning attitude.
3.  **Ethics & Society:** To equip learners with ethical responsibilities towards society and the environment along with leadership skills.

## 5. Laboratories & Infrastructure
* **Search Tags:** **MECH_LABS, THERMAL_LAB, WORKSHOP, PRODUCTION_LAB, CAD_LAB.**
* **Overview:** The department features **10+ specialized labs** including a dedicated **Project Lab** for innovation.

### Key Laboratories
1.  **Thermal Engineering Lab:** Internal Combustion (IC) Engine testing and performance analysis.
2.  **Machine Tools Lab:** Operation of Lathe, Milling, and Drilling machines.
3.  **CAD/CAM Lab:** Equipped with **SolidWorks**, **ANSYS (16.0)**, **CATIA (V5)**, and **AutoCAD (2023)**.
4.  **Heat Transfer Lab:** Experiments on conduction, convection, and radiation.
5.  **Fluid Mechanics & Hydraulic Machines:** Testing of pumps and turbines.
6.  **Production Technology Lab:** Hands-on exposure to manufacturing processes.
7.  **Robotics Lab:** (Planned/In-development) Focus on Humanoid Robotics and Automation.
