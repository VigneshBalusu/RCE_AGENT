## Accreditation Status (Quality Seals)

### NAAC Accreditation
* **SearchTags:** **NAAC, NAAC_GRADE, NAAC_A+, QUALITY_SEAL, ACCREDITATION_STATUS.**
* **Grade:** **A+ (A Plus)**
* **Significance:** The highest seal of quality for teaching, infrastructure, and governance.

### NBA Accreditation (Program Specific)
* **SearchTags:** **NBA, NBA_ACCREDITED_COURSES, NBA_PROGRAMS, NBA_BRANCHES, COURSE_ACCREDITATION.**
* **Definition:** The **National Board of Accreditation (NBA)** has accredited select core engineering programs.
* **Accredited Programs List:**
  1. **Computer Science & Engineering (CSE)**
  2. **Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE)**
  3. **Electrical & Electronics Engineering (EEE)**
  4. **Mechanical Engineering (ME)**
  5. **Civil Engineering (CE)**
* **Note:** Newer branches (AI-DS, IoT) are not yet eligible as they are recently established.

## Regulatory Approvals
* **SearchTags:** **APPROVALS, AUTONOMY, UGC, AICTE, JNTUK, AFFILIATION, UNIVERSITY_STATUS.**
* **Autonomy:** Conferred **Autonomous Status** by **UGC** (University Grants Commission).
* **Approvals:** All courses are approved by **AICTE**, New Delhi.
* **Affiliation:** Permanently affiliated to **JNTU Kakinada (JNTUK)**.
