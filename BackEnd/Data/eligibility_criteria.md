# Eligibility Criteria - Ramachandra College of Engineering (RCEE)

## Overview of Eligibility
* **SearchTags:** **ELIGIBILITY_OVERVIEW, ADMISSION_REQUIREMENTS, RCEE_CRITERIA, QUALIFICATION_NORMS, ENTRANCE_EXAMS_REQUIRED.**
* **Content:** This document outlines the academic and entrance exam requirements for admission into Undergraduate (B.Tech) and Postgraduate (MBA, M.Tech) programs at Ramachandra College of Engineering, Eluru.

---

## 1. B.Tech Eligibility (Regular 4-Year Course)
* **SearchTags:** **BTECH_ELIGIBILITY, INTERMEDIATE_REQUIREMENTS, MPC_MARKS, EAMCET_RANK, MANAGEMENT_QUOTA_ELIGIBILITY.**
* **Academic Requirements:**
    * **Education:** Passed 10+2 (Intermediate) examination conducted by the Board of Intermediate Education, Govt. of Andhra Pradesh, or an equivalent examination.
    * **Subjects:** Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry (MPC) as optional subjects.
    * **Minimum Marks (General):** **45%** aggregate marks in group subjects.
    * **Minimum Marks (SC/ST/BC):** **40%** aggregate marks in group subjects.
* **Entrance Exam Requirements:**
    * **Convener Quota (Category A):** Must qualify in **AP EAMCET** (EAPCET) and participate in web counseling.
    * **Management Quota (Category B):** Admissions are based on merit in **JEE Main**, **AP EAMCET** rank, or Intermediate (10+2) marks.

---

## 2. B.Tech Lateral Entry Eligibility (For Diploma Holders)
* **SearchTags:** **LATERAL_ENTRY, DIPLOMA_TO_BTECH, ECET_ELIGIBILITY, 2ND_YEAR_ADMISSION, POLYTECHNIC_ADMISSION.**
* **Academic Requirements:**
    * **Qualification:** Must hold a 3-year Diploma in Engineering/Technology awarded by the State Board of Technical Education, Andhra Pradesh (or equivalent).
    * **Minimum Marks (General):** **45%** aggregate in Diploma.
    * **Minimum Marks (Reserved):** **40% aggregate** in Diploma.
* **Entrance Exam Requirements:**
    * Must qualify in **AP ECET** (Engineering Common Entrance Test).
    * **Seat Availability:** 10% of total intake is reserved for Lateral Entry (Supernumerary seats).

## 4. M.Tech Eligibility (Postgraduate)
* **SearchTags:** **MTECH_ELIGIBILITY, GATE_SCORE, PGECET_RANK, BTECH_SPECIALIZATION, MTECH_ADMISSION, POSTGRAD_ENGINEERING.**
* **Academic Requirements:**
    * **Education:** B.E./B.Tech degree in the relevant discipline (e.g., CSE, ECE, EEE, Mech).

* **Entrance Exam Requirements:**

## Age Criteria
* **SearchTags:** **AGE_LIMIT, ADMISSION_AGE, MINIMUM_AGE, APSCHE_AGE_RULES, BTECH_AGE_CRITERIA.**
* **General Rule:** Candidates should have completed **16 years** of age by the date of commencement of admission for B.Tech.
* **Upper Limit:** There is no upper age limit for the qualifying examination.
* **AI Note:** Use Web Search to check "AP EAMCET age limit [Current Year]" for specific cutoffs or updates.

## Accepted Entrance Exams Summary
* **SearchTags:** **EXAM_LIST, ENTRANCE_TESTS, EAPCET, ECET, ICET, PGECET, GATE, JEE_MAIN, EXAM_SUMMARY.**
* **AP EAMCET (EAPCET):** For 1st Year B.Tech.
* **AP ECET:** For 2nd Year B.Tech (Lateral Entry).
* **AP ICET:** For MBA.
* **GATE / AP PGECET:** For M.Tech.
* **JEE Main:** Accepted for Management Quota (Category B) seats only.
