## 5. Laboratories & Infrastructure
* **Search Tags:** **CIVIL_LABS, SURVEY_LAB, CONCRETE_LAB, CIVIL_INFRASTRUCTURE, HYDRAULICS_LAB.**
* **Overview:** The department is equipped with state-of-the-art testing equipment for soil, concrete, and survey work.

### Key Laboratories
1.  **Surveying Lab:** Equipped with **Total Stations**, Theodolites, and Dumpy levels.
2.  **Concrete Technology Lab:** Facilities for testing cement, aggregates, and concrete strength (Compression Testing Machine).
3.  **Geotechnical / Soil Mechanics Lab:** Direct shear test apparatus, Sieve shakers, and soil testing kits.
4.  **Transportation Engineering Lab:** Testing of highway materials (Bitumen, Aggregate impact value).
5.  **Environmental Engineering Lab:** Water quality testing and analysis.
6.  **CAD / GIS Lab:** Computer Aided Design (AutoCAD) and Geographic Information Systems (ArcGIS).
7.  **Strength of Materials (SM) Lab:** Universal Testing Machine (UTM) for steel and other materials.
8.  **Engineering Geology Lab:** Identification of minerals and rocks.
