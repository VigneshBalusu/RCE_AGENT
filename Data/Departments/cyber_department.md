## 2. Vision & Mission (Cyber Security)
* **Search Tags:** **CYBER_VISION, CYBER_MISSION, CS_GOALS.**

### Vision
"To carry out extensive awareness on the dangers of the internet and train students to **detect, analyze, and prevent cyber threats** to protect national cyber infrastructure."

### Mission
1.  **Skill Building:** To build proficient skills necessary to pursue a career in Cyber Security through **Virtual Labs** and advanced tools.
2.  **Real-World Defense:** To develop strategies and policies to counter cybercrime using **real-time projects**.
3.  **Ethics:** To instill professional ethics and legal knowledge regarding cyber laws and data privacy.

## 4. Academic Focus & Curriculum
* **Search Tags:** **CYBER_SYLLABUS, CS_SUBJECTS, CYBER_CURRICULUM.**
* **Core Subjects:**
    * **Cryptography:** Encryption algorithms (AES, RSA), Digital Signatures.
    * **Network Security:** Firewalls, VPNs, Intrusion Detection Systems (IDS).
    * **Ethical Hacking:** Penetration testing, Vulnerability assessment.
    * **Cyber Forensics:** Digital evidence recovery and analysis.
    * **Blockchain Technology:** Secure decentralized systems.

## 5. Laboratories & Infrastructure
* **Search Tags:** **CYBER_LABS, SECURITY_LAB, HACKING_LAB, VIRTUAL_LAB.**
* **Overview:** The department features a unique **Virtual Cyber Security Laboratory** that simulates cyber-attacks for training.

### Key Laboratories
1.  **Virtual Cyber Security Lab:** A sandboxed environment for practicing **Ethical Hacking** and defense without risking the actual network.
2.  **Network Security Lab:** Equipped with **Cisco Packet Tracer** and Wireshark for analyzing network traffic.
3.  **Cryptography Lab:** Focus on implementing encryption algorithms using Python/Java.
4.  **Unix/Linux Lab:** Essential for learning security command-line tools (Kali Linux environment).
5.  **Project Lab:** High-performance systems for running heavy security simulations.
