## 5. Laboratories & Infrastructure
* **Search Tags:** **ECE_LABS, VLSI_LAB, DSP_LAB, EMBEDDED_LAB, ECE_INFRASTRUCTURE.**
* **Overview:** The department has **8+ specialized laboratories** equipped with licensed software like **Xilinx**, **Mentor Graphics**, and **MATLAB**.

### Key Laboratories
1.  **VLSI Design Lab:** Equipped with **Xilinx ISE**, **Mentor Graphics**, and **Spartan-3 FPGA Kits** for chip design.
2.  **Microprocessors & Microcontrollers Lab:** Features **8086 & 8051 kits**, Keil µVision, and ARM processors for embedded coding.
3.  **Digital Signal Processing (DSP) Lab:** Equipped with **TMS320C6713 DSP Development kits** and Code Composer Studio.
4.  **Electronic Devices & Circuits (EDC) Lab:** Foundation lab with CROs, Function Generators, and Regulated Power Supplies.
5.  **AC & DC Communications Lab:** For analyzing analog signals using Spectrum Analyzers and **MULTISIM** software.
6.  **STLD / PLC Lab:** Advanced lab for Programmable Logic Controllers (PLC) and automation logic.
7.  **Research & Development (R&D) Lab:** Dedicated space for faculty and student innovation projects.
