# Freshman Engineering Department (FED) - Overview

## Scope of Document
This document details the profile, academic focus, and infrastructure of the Freshman Engineering Department (FED), also known as Science & Humanities (S&H), at Ramachandra College of Engineering (RCEE).
**Use this data for:** "Who is FED HOD?", "First Year Syllabus", "Induction Program", "Parichay Event", "English Lab", "I Year HOD", "S&H Department".

## 1. Department Profile & Stats
* **Department Name:** **Freshman Engineering Department (FED)**
* **Alternate Name:** **Science & Humanities (S&H)**
* **Scope:** Handles the **First Year (I-Year)** curriculum for **ALL B.Tech Branches** (CSE, ECE, EEE, Mech, Civil, AI&DS, etc.).
* **Search Tags:** **FED_PROFILE, FIRST_YEAR, FRESHMAN, SH_DEPT, INDUCTION_PROGRAM.**
* **Key Role:** Acts as a bridge between Intermediate/10+2 education and Core Engineering, focusing on fundamental sciences and discipline.

## 5. Laboratories & Infrastructure
* **Search Tags:** **FED_LABS, ENGLISH_LAB, PHYSICS_LAB, CHEMISTRY_LAB, WORKSHOP.**
* **Overview:** The department maintains **state-of-the-art basic science labs** to ensure strong fundamentals.

### Key Laboratories
1.  **English Communication Skills (ELCS) Lab:**
    * **Software:** Licensed **"K-Van Solutions"** and **"Globerena"** software.
    * **Focus:** Listening, Speaking, Reading, and Writing (LSRW) skills.
2.  **Engineering Physics Lab:** Experiments on optics, lasers, and semiconductors.
3.  **Engineering Chemistry Lab:** Water analysis, titrations, and chemical testing.
4.  **Engineering Workshop:** Hands-on training in Carpentry, Fitting, House Wiring, and Tin Smithy.
5.  **IT Workshop:** PC Hardware assembly, OS Installation, and MS Office training.

## 6. Student Support & Counseling
* **Search Tags:** **FED_COUNSELING, MENTORING_SYSTEM, PARENT_COMMUNICATION.**
* **Counseling System:**
    * **Ratio:** Every **20-30 students** are assigned a dedicated Faculty Mentor.
    * **Role:** Monitors attendance, academic progress, and disciplinary issues.
* **Communication:** Regular updates to parents regarding attendance and exam marks via SMS/Phone.
* **Discipline:** FED is known for strict enforcement of discipline (Dress code, ID cards, Punctuality) to groom professionals.

## 7. Key Events: "PARICHAY" (Induction Program)
* **Search Tags:** **PARICHAY, ORIENTATION_DAY, INDUCTION_PROGRAM, FRESHERS_DAY.**
* **Event Name:** **"PARICHAY"** (Annual Orientation Program).
* **Purpose:** To welcome new students and parents, introducing them to the college culture, autonomous rules, and placement achievements.
* **Activities:**
    * **Zero Semester:** A 2-3 week **Induction Program** covering Yoga, Creative Arts, Universal Human Values (UHV), and Literary activities.
    * **Guest Lectures:** Motivational talks by eminent personalities to inspire freshers.
