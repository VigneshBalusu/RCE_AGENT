# Academic Regulations & Examination Rules - Ramachandra College of Engineering (RCEE)

## Scope of Document
* **SearchTags:** **ACADEMIC_RULES, EXAM_RULES, REGULATIONS_OVERVIEW, RCEE_GUIDELINES.**
* **Summary:** This document outlines the academic rules (R23/R20), attendance norms, grading systems, and exam patterns.
* **Usage:** Use this data for questions about "Pass Marks", "Attendance Shortage", "Credits", and "Backlogs".

## 1. Applicable Regulations (Batch-Wise)
* **SearchTags:** **R23_REGULATION, R20_REGULATION, BATCH_RULES, SYLLABUS_NORMS.**
* **R23 Regulation:** Applicable for students admitted from **2023 onwards** (Current 1st & 2nd Years).
* **R20 Regulation:** Applicable for senior batches (Current 3rd & 4th Years).
* **AI Note:** If the year is unclear, default to R23 but mention: "As per latest R23 norms...".

## 2. Attendance Requirements
* **SearchTags:** **ATTENDANCE, ATTENDANCE_SHORTAGE, CONDONATION, DETENTION, MEDICAL_LEAVE.**
* **Minimum Target:** **75%** aggregate attendance is mandatory to appear for End Exams.
* **Condonation (Medical):** Shortage between **65% and 75%** may be condoned with a Medical Certificate and fee.
* **Detention:** Attendance **below 65%** generally leads to detention (must repeat the semester).

## 3. Pass Criteria & Grading (10-Point Scale)
* **SearchTags:** **PASS_MARKS, PASS_CRITERIA, GRADING_SYSTEM, INTERNAL_MARKS, EXTERNAL_MARKS.**
* **Condition 1:** Minimum **35%** marks in the External (End-Semester) Exam.
* **Condition 2:** Minimum **40%** marks in Total (Internal + External combined).

## 4. Examination Pattern
* **SearchTags:** **MID_EXAMS, END_EXAMS, SUPPLEMENTARY_EXAMS, SUPPLY_EXAMS, INTERNAL_ASSESSMENT.**
* **Internals:** Conducted twice per semester; includes descriptive and objective tests.
* **End-Sem:** 3-hour duration exams conducted at the end of the term.
* **Supplementary:** For students who fail; usually conducted alongside regular exams.

## 5. Credit Structure (B.Tech)
* **SearchTags:** **CREDITS, TOTAL_CREDITS, DEGREE_REQUIREMENTS, BTECH_CREDITS.**
* **Total Credits:** **160 Credits** are required for the award of a B.Tech Degree.
* **Distribution:** Includes Basic Sciences, Professional Core, Open Electives, and Projects.

## 6. Promotion Rules (Critical)
* **SearchTags:** **PROMOTION_RULES, CREDIT_DETENTION, ACADEMIC_PROMOTION, DETENTION_RULES.**
* **Year 1 to 2:** Based on attendance only.
* **Year 2 to 3:** Must secure **50% of total credits** up to 2nd Year 1st Sem.
* **Year 3 to 4:** Must secure **50% of total credits** up to 3rd Year 1st Sem.
