# Admissions Process - Ramachandra College of Engineering (RCEE)

## Overview of RCEE Admissions
* **SearchTags:** **ADMISSIONS, ENROLLMENT, JOIN_RCEE, ADMISSION_GUIDE, ADMISSION_PROCESS, RCEE_STEPS.**
* **Content:** This document explains the admission process for Ramachandra College of Engineering (RCEE), Eluru. It covers entrance exams, counseling, documents required, and official links.

## Admission Pathways & Quotas
* **SearchTags:** **CATEGORY_A, CATEGORY_B, GOVERNMENT_QUOTA, MANAGEMENT_QUOTA, ADMISSION_QUOTA.**
* **Quotas:**
    * AP EAMCET (Category A – Government Quota)
    * Management Quota (Category B – Direct Admission)

## AP EAMCET Counseling Code
* **SearchTags:** **COUNSELING_CODE, RACE_CODE, EAMCET_CODE, EAPCET_CODE, COLLEGE_CODE.**
* **Official Code:** The AP EAMCET counseling code for Ramachandra College of Engineering is: **RACE**

## Step 1: Admission Eligibility Criteria
* **SearchTags:** **ELIGIBILITY, ADMISSION_REQUIREMENTS, BTECH_ELIGIBILITY, MPC_MARKS.**
* **Criteria:**
    * Candidate must complete 10+2 with Mathematics, Physics, and Chemistry.
    * Must qualify AP EAMCET for Category A admissions.
    * Category B candidates must meet minimum academic eligibility.

## Step 2: Entrance Examination (AP EAMCET)
* **SearchTags:** **EAMCET_EXAM, APSCHE_PORTAL, EAPCET_RANK, ENTRANCE_TEST.**
* **Process:**
    * Register for AP EAMCET (EAPCET) through the official APSCHE portal.
    * Appear for the examination and obtain a valid rank.

## Step 3: Counseling Registration Process
* **SearchTags:** **COUNSELING_REGISTRATION, CERTIFICATE_VERIFICATION, REGISTRATION_FEE.**
* **Process:**
    * Register on the AP EAMCET counseling portal.
    * Pay counseling registration fee and complete certificate verification.

## Step 4: Choice Filling (Web Options)
* **SearchTags:** **WEB_OPTIONS, CHOICE_FILLING, LOCK_CHOICES, OPTION_ENTRY.**
* **Process:**
    * Select Ramachandra College of Engineering using code **RACE**.
    * **Critical:** Lock choices before the deadline.

## Step 5: Seat Allotment Results
* **SearchTags:** **SEAT_ALLOTMENT, ALLOTMENT_ORDER, ALLOTMENT_RESULTS.**
* **Process:**
    * Seat allotment results are published online by APSCHE.
    * Download the allotment order immediately after confirmation.

## Step 6: Reporting to College
* **SearchTags:** **COLLEGE_REPORTING, ADMISSION_FEES, CAMPUS_ADMISSION, DOCUMENT_VERIFICATION.**
* **Process:**
    * Report to RCEE campus with the allotment order.
    * Complete document verification and pay tuition/admission fees.

## Documents Required for Counseling
* **SearchTags:** **REQUIRED_DOCUMENTS, ADMISSION_CHECKLIST, CERTIFICATES, TC, MARKS_MEMO.**
* **Required Files:**
    * AP EAMCET Hall Ticket & Rank Card
    * 10th (SSC) & Intermediate (10+2) Marks Memos
    * Transfer Certificate (TC) & Caste/Income Certificates (if applicable)
    * Aadhaar Card & Passport-size photographs
