# Campus Infrastructure & Facilities - Ramachandra College of Engineering (RCEE)

## Scope of Document
* **SearchTags:** **INFRASTRUCTURE_OVERVIEW, CAMPUS_FACILITIES, AMENITIES_GUIDE, RCEE_RESOURCES, CAMPUS_MAP_INFO.**

* **Exclusions:** Do NOT use for Hostel rooms (See File 06) or Bus routes (See File 06).

## 1. Laboratories (Academic & Research)
* **SearchTags:** **LABS, LABORATORIES, RESEARCH_LABS, COMPUTER_LABS, ECE_LABS, CSE_LABS, MECHANICAL_LABS, CIVIL_LABS, PRACTICAL_EXPOSURE.**
* **Details:** RCEE features **70+ state-of-the-art laboratories** ensuring practical exposure.
* **Core Departments:** Specialized labs for ECE (VLSI), EEE (Power Systems), Mechanical (CNC/Workshop), and Civil (Surveying).
* **Computer Labs:** High-configuration systems supporting CSE, AI-DS, and Cyber Security.
* **Usage:** Open for regular coursework, mini-projects, and research.

## 2. Central Library (Knowledge Resource Center)
* **SearchTags:** **LIBRARY, BOOKS, E-JOURNALS, DIGITAL_LIBRARY, NPTEL, READING_HALL, STUDY_RESOURCES, IEEE_SPRINGER.**
* **Content:** The Central Library serves as the academic hub of the campus.
* **Volumes:** **27,300+** Books.
* **Digital Resources:** Subscription to National/International e-Journals (IEEE, Springer).
* **Facilities:** Reading hall, Digital Library for online research, and NPTEL access.

## 3. Innovation Ecosystem (R&D)
* **SearchTags:** **IDEA_LAB, STARTUPS, INNOVATION, ENTREPRENEURSHIP, IEDC, PATENTS, PRODUCT_DEVELOPMENT, INCUBATION.**
* **AICTE IDEA Lab:** A government-funded lab for prototyping and product development.
* **IEDC (Innovation & Entrepreneurship Development Cell):** Supports student startups and patent filing.

## 4. Sports & Fitness
* **SearchTags:** **SPORTS, GYM, FITNESS, GAMES, CRICKET, VOLLEYBALL, BASKETBALL, INDOOR_GAMES, FOOTBALL, SHUTTLE.**
* **Outdoor Sports:** Cricket ground, Volleyball courts, Football field, Basketball court.
* **Indoor Games:** Table Tennis (TT), Carrom, Chess, Shuttle Badminton.
* **Gymnasium:** Fully equipped **Gym/Fitness Center** available for students (Timings separate for boys/girls).

## 5. Canteen & Cafeteria
* **SearchTags:** **CANTEEN, FOOD, LUNCH, CAFETERIA, SNACKS, HYGIENE, DINING, BREAKFAST, SOUTH_INDIAN_FOOD.**
* **Facility:** Large, hygienic on-campus canteen.
* **Menu:** Affordable breakfast, lunch, snacks, and beverages (South & North Indian options).
* **Hygiene:** Maintains high standards of food safety.

## 6. General Campus Amenities
* **SearchTags:** **WIFI, AUDITORIUM, SEMINAR_HALLS, MEDICAL_FACILITY, HEALTH_CENTER, DOCTOR, EMERGENCY_VEHICLE, FIRST_AID.**
* **Wi-Fi:** **24/7 Wi-Fi connectivity** across the campus (Academic blocks & Labs).
* **Auditorium:** Air-conditioned seminar halls and a main auditorium for events, workshops, and guest lectures.
* **Medical Facility:** On-campus **Health Center** with first-aid facilities and a visiting doctor. Emergency vehicle available.
