# Fee Structure - Ramachandra College of Engineering (RCEE)

## 1. Annual Tuition Fee (B.Tech, M.Tech & MBA)
Fees are categorized based on the admission quota and strictly follow APSCHE guidelines.

* **Category A (Convener Quota):**
    * **Governing Body:** Fixed by the Government of Andhra Pradesh (APSCHE).
    * **Estimated Fee:** Approximately **₹60,200 per annum** (Verify for the current academic year).
* **Category B (Management Quota):**
    * Determined by the college management based on branch demand and merit.

## 2. Hostel Fees (Boys & Girls)
Residential facilities are available on campus with separate blocks for men and women.

* **Estimated Cost:** **₹60,000 – ₹90,000 per year**.
* **Variations:** Cost depends on room type (AC vs. Non-AC) and sharing configurations.
* **Inclusions:** Accommodation, Mess (Food), Wi-Fi, and general amenities.
* **Payment Mode:** Annual or semester-wise options may be available.
* **Search Tag:** `RCEE Eluru hostel fee structure and facilities 2025`

---

## 3. Transport Fees (Bus)
Optional transportation services are available for day scholars.

* **Coverage:** Eluru, Vijayawada, and surrounding towns.
* **Fee Determinant:** Charges vary based on the boarding point distance and specific route.
* **Billing:** Charged separately from tuition and hostel fees.
*  **Search Tag:** `Ramachandra College of Engineering bus transport fee chart 2025`

## 4. Caution Deposit
* **Type:** Refundable.
* **Collection:** Collected once at the time of admission.
* **Refund Policy:** Returned at the end of the course duration, subject to clearance of dues/damages.
* **Search Tag:** `RCEE Eluru admission caution deposit details`

---

## 5. Fee Reimbursement (Government Schemes)
Eligible students may avail of tuition fee waivers under Andhra Pradesh state schemes.

* **Schemes:** **Jagananna Vidya Deevena (JVD)** (Full Fee Reimbursement) & **Vasathi Deevena** (Hostel/Food allowance).
* **Eligibility Criteria:**
    * **Income:** Family annual income < **₹2.5 Lakhs**.
    * **Quota:** Admission must be through **Category A (Convener)**.
    * **Attendance:** Minimum **75%** attendance is mandatory to maintain eligibility.
    * **Documentation:** Valid Income and Caste Certificates required.
*  **Search Tag:** `AP Jagananna Vidya Deevena eligibility guidelines 2025`

---

## 6. Ramachandra Merit Scholarship
The college allocates a scholarship pool of **₹50 Lakhs** to reward academic excellence.

* **Target Group:** Top rankers in EAPCET/ICET and consistent academic toppers.
* **Incentives:**
    * Tuition or Hostel fee concessions.
    * **Laptops:** Potential reward for top-ranking students.
* **Search Tag:** `Ramachandra College of Engineering merit scholarship criteria`

---

## 7. Payment Schedule & Additional Charges
* **Tuition:** Payable annually at the commencement of the academic year.
* **Exam Fees:** Payable every semester prior to examinations.
* **Penalties:** Late payments attract fines as per college regulations.
*  **Search Tag:** `RCEE Eluru examination fee notification and payment dates`

### Important Disclaimer
All fees and reimbursement policies are subject to change based on directives from **APSCHE (Andhra Pradesh State Council of Higher Education)** and **AICTE**. Students are strongly advised to verify the latest "Fee Notification Circular" directly from the RCEE Administrative Office.
