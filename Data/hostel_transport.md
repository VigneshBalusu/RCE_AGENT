## 3. Hostel Facilities (Boys & Girls)
RCEE provides **separate hostels** for boys and girls within the campus perimeter, designed to provide a secure and home-like environment.

### Room Configuration
* **Sharing:** Rooms are generally configured for **3-sharing** or **4-sharing**.
* **Furniture:** Every student is provided with a cot, cupboard, study table, and chair.

### Amenities & Utilities
* **Connectivity:** High-speed **Wi-Fi** is available for academic needs.
* **Water:** 24/7 Purified **RO Mineral Water** supply.
* **Power:** Uninterrupted power supply via backup generators.
* **Recreation:** Common TV Hall, Indoor Games facilities, and Gym access.

### Mess & Dining
* **Menu:** The mess serves both **Vegetarian & Non-Vegetarian** options.
* **Schedule:** Breakfast, Lunch, Evening Snacks, and Dinner are provided.

## 4. Campus Safety & Mandatory Disclosures
RCEE prioritizes student safety and adheres to all statutory norms.

* **Anti-Ragging:** A strict **Zero-Tolerance Policy** is enforced. An Anti-Ragging committee actively monitors hostels and transport.
* **AICTE Approved:** All courses are approved by the All India Council for Technical Education.
* **IQAC:** The Internal Quality Assurance Cell monitors academic standards.
* **NIRF:** The college participates in the National Institutional Ranking Framework.
* **RTI:** Information is accessible under the Right to Information Act.
* **NSS:** A National Service Scheme unit is active for student volunteering.
