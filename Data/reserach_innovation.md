# Research, Innovation & Startups - Ramachandra College of Engineering (RCEE)

## 1. Research & Development (R&D) Cell
RCEE maintains a robust R&D ecosystem headed by **Dr. Jarabala Ranga**, aimed at fostering cutting-edge technological advancements.

* **Focus Areas:** Artificial Intelligence (AI), Electric Vehicles (EV), Smart Grids, Internet of Things (IoT), and Blockchain Technology.
* **Key Achievements:**
    * **Patents:** Faculty and students have successfully filed **20+ Indian Patents**.
    * **International Recognition:** The college has secured **5+ UK Grants**.
    * **Publications:** Active research is frequently published in prestigious **IEEE Xplore** and **SCI journals**.

## 2. Innovation Centers & Labs
The college hosts state-of-the-art facilities to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and industrial prototyping.

* **AICTE IDEA Lab:**
    * **Grant:** Funded by AICTE with a substantial grant of approximately **₹1.10 Crore**.
    * **Purpose:** A dedicated manufacturing and prototyping facility open for students to convert theoretical ideas into physical products.
* **NewGen IEDC & IIC:**
    * **Institution’s Innovation Council (IIC):** Provides funding support, mentorship, and a platform for budding student entrepreneurs.
