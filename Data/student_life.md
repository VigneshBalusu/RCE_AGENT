# Student Life, Clubs & Cultural Events - Ramachandra College of Engineering (RCEE)

## 1. Annual Festivals (Fests)
* **SearchTags:** **FESTS, CULTURAL_ACTIVITIES, KREYA, PARICHAY, FRESHERS_DAY, SANKRANTHI_SAMBARALU, TECH_FESTS, CAMPUS_EVENTS, COLLEGE_FEST.**
* **Overview:** RCEE is known for its vibrant campus life, highlighted by several major annual celebrations.
* **KREYA:** The flagship **National Level Cultural & Technical Fest**.
    * *Highlights:* Dance, Music, Tech Expos, and Fashion Shows.
    * *Timing:* Typically held in **March or April**.
* **Parichay:** The official **Freshers' Day** celebration to welcome new students.
* **Sankranthi Sambaralu:** A grand traditional celebration featuring Rangoli competitions and ethnic wear.
* **Tech Fests:** Department-specific technical symposia (e.g., "Talent Explore" by CSE).

## 2. Student Clubs
* **SearchTags:** **CLUBS, STUDENT_CLUBS, ROBOTICS_CLUB, CODING_CLUB, CULTURAL_CLUBS, WOMEN_EMPOWERMENT, IETE, FOSS.**
* **Overview:** RCEE hosts a variety of active clubs designed to foster holistic student development beyond academics.

### Technical Clubs
* **Robotics Club:** Conducts hands-on workshops on Arduino and IoT.
* **Coding Club (FOSS):** Focuses on Open Source Software contribution and Hackathon preparation.
* **IETE Student Forum:** Specialized workshops for Electronics and Communication.

### Cultural & Social Clubs
* **Rani Rudrama Devi Club:** Dedicated to promoting culture, arts, and traditions.
* **Mother Theresa Social Club:** Focuses on charity, empathy, and social service initiatives.
* **Kalpana Chawla Women Empowerment Cell:** Organizes events and workshops specifically for female students and staff.

## 3. Social Service (NSS)
* **SearchTags:** **NSS, SOCIAL_SERVICE, VOLUNTEERING, BLOOD_DONATION, SWACHH_BHARAT, MEDICAL_CAMPS, COMMUNITY_SERVICE.**
* **Overview:** The college has a highly active **National Service Scheme (NSS)** unit that encourages community engagement.
* **Activities:** Regular Blood Donation Camps, Village Adoption programs, Swachh Bharat drives, and Medical Camps.
* **Student Benefit:** Participants earn social credits and certificates, adding value to their overall profile.

## 4. Professional Chapters
* **SearchTags:** **IEEE, CSI, PROFESSIONAL_NETWORKS, GLOBAL_CHAPTERS, COMPUTER_SOCIETY, ENGINEERING_NETWORKS.**
* **Overview:** Students have access to global and national professional networks.
* **IEEE Student Branch:** Connects students with the global engineering community for networking and standards.
* **CSI (Computer Society of India):** Focuses on professional development for CSE and IT students.
